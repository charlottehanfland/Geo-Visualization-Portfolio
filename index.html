Seasonal, Recreational, and Occassionally Occupied Vacant Properties by County (2020 Census)

This choropleth map depicts the percentage of vacant homes in each county that are empty due to being used seasonally/recreationally or only occasionally for various reasons. The goal of this map was to see if there was a correlation between vacation destinations and temporarily vacant housing.

Maximum Output of US Nuclear Power Plants (Megawatts)

Using proportional symbols, this map depicts nuclear power plants by their maximum output in megawatts. There are no noticable outliers and the spatial pattern of nuclear power plant sites was more interesting than varying maximum capacities. Going forward, I am developing a similar proportional symbol map showing the capacity and output of wind power plants across the country.

Solo Living Women with Master's Degrees

A bivariate map depicting the percentage of single female householders against the percentage of women with master's degrees by state. The goal of this map was to see if there are any areas where women living alone correlate with areas where a greater percentage of educated people are women with master's degrees.
